# Dot UI/UX tiep theo

## Da sua trong dot nay

- User chua dang nhap se vao thang man hinh dang nhap/dang ky rieng.
- Sidebar, menu, source drawer va cac trang san pham khong hien truoc khi co session.
- Tai khoan role `user` khong con thay muc van hanh/admin.
- Man hinh dang nhap di theo huong thiet ke trong `D:\TravelAssistant\UI`: split layout co anh, form gon, logo TravelAssistant dung san pham, khong co Facebook login.
- Mobile da khoa lai khoang cach mep trai/phai, input/button/tab khong tran khoi viewport.

## Viec UI con can lam

- Thay anh nen Unsplash bang asset so huu rieng hoac CDN cua du an.
- Kiem tra va sua cac chu tieng Viet bi mojibake trong nhung man hinh cu.
- Them first-run onboarding sau khi user dang ky.
- Them loading state than thien khi backend Render bi cold start.
- Chi hien giai thich "chua co nguon" sau khi cau tra loi that su khong co nguon, khong hien truoc cau hoi dau tien.
- Toi uu man hinh voice cho thao tac mot tay tren dien thoai.
- Them UI ban do khi map/route that da duoc ket noi.
- Them man hinh cai dat tai khoan: ho so, so thich, wishlist.

## Tieu chi chap nhan

- Lan dau vao web chi thay dang nhap/dang ky.
- Dang nhap/dang ky vua desktop va mobile, khong bi cuon doc qua nhieu.
- Role `user` thay chat, planner, voice, explore, trips, account.
- Role `editor`, `admin`, `root` thay them van hanh.
- Khong con nut Facebook hay copy social login.
- Dang nhap thanh cong thi vao workspace tro ly.
